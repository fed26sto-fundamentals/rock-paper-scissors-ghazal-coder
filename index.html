How to Play Rock, Paper, Scissors

Rock, Paper, Scissors is a simple hand game played between two people where each player simultaneously forms one of three shapes with their hand.

Step 1: Learn the Rules

Each of the three possible shapes (Rock, Paper, or Scissors) defeats one of the other two and loses to the other.

Step 2: Learn the Moves

Rock beats scissors, scissors beats paper, and paper beats rock.

Step 3: Start Playing

Both players say "rock, paper, scissors" and then reveal their choice simultaneously.
